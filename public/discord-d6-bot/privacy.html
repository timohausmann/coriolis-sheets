Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord D6 Bot ("the Service") handles data.

Who operates this bot

What data is collected

The bot does not collect or store personal data such as:

  • Usernames
  • User IDs
  • Server names
  • Message content beyond command execution
  • IP addresses

For technical operation only, the following information may be logged:

  • Executed bot commands (e.g. dice roll syntax)
  • Generated dice results
  • Error messages and stack traces
  • Service start/stop events and performance metrics

These logs are used exclusively for debugging, monitoring, maintenance, and improving the Service.

Legal basis (GDPR / DSGVO)

Processing is based on Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) to ensure stability, security, and correct operation of the Service.

Data sharing

Logged data is not shared with third parties and is not sold, rented, or used for advertising.

Data retention

Logs are retained only as long as necessary for operational purposes and may be deleted automatically or manually at any time.

Your rights

If you believe the Service processes personal data related to you, you have the right to request information or deletion. In practice, the bot is designed to avoid storing such data in the first place.

Children

The Service does not knowingly collect data from children. Discord’s own age restrictions apply.
